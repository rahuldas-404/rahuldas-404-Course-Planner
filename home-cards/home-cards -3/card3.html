Safety

Safety is paramount to our success as students, staff and faculty. We are fortunate to have a community committed to safety. This commitment is evident in the high quality programs and services offered to keep our campus safe 24/7, 365 days a year. These services include our University.

Health

Being a successful student means more than just getting good grades. Success at the VTU includes maintaining your health and making healthy choices that support your academic, social and career goals. Our campus offers a variety of health education and support services to help you be, get, and stay healthy.

Wellness

Wellness includes much more than physical health. Wellness address your emotional well being, your environment, academics, harmony between your work and social life, your finances, and your spiritual health.
